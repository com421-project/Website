MeterSave

A program offered by the Chicago Department of Water Management to Chicago where you can save water by participating in MeterSave program. Homeowners without water meters can voluntarily install a meter in their home.
  • Enroll for the program at https://www.metersave.org
  • After you have registered for MeterSave, a representative will call you to schedule your free DWM water meter installation.
  • Sign the consent prior to meter installation
  • You may choose to have your water tested prior to the installation.
  • Voter Registration in Illinois: https://www.elections.il.gov/votinginformation/register.aspx
  • Find Your Representative: https://www.house.gov/representatives/find-your-representative
